# Stakeholder Alignment Map (RASCI Matrix)

Driving alignment in a high-stakes, fast-paced environment means knowing exactly who owns the decision and who needs to be consulted to prevent downstream bottlenecks.

## Legend
*   **R - Responsible:** The person who performs the work to complete the task.
*   **A - Accountable:** The person with final decision-making power and veto authority (only one per task).
*   **C - Consulted:** Subject matter experts whose input is required before the task is finalized.
*   **I - Informed:** Parties kept up-to-date on progress or outcomes.
